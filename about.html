About StoryCast

StoryCast is an interactive microsite designed to showcase the power of audio and video storytelling. This specific edition is dedicated to the legendary Brazilian soccer player, Ronaldinho Gaúcho. Our goal is to bring his magical moments to life through immersive, accessible media.

Our Commitment to Accessibility

StoryCast was built with inclusivity at its core. We strive to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 AA standards. Below are some of the key features we have implemented to ensure a seamless experience for all users:

  • Semantic HTML: The site uses proper structural elements to convey meaning to assistive technologies.
  • Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements, including media players and navigation links, are fully accessible via keyboard. A "Skip to main content" link is available for screen reader and keyboard users.
  • Visible Focus States: We have implemented custom, high-contrast focus rings for keyboard navigation.
  • Media Transcripts & Captions: All video and audio stories are accompanied by detailed text transcripts, and videos utilize <track> elements for captions.
  • Color Contrast: Our color palette has been carefully selected and tested to ensure sufficient contrast ratios for text and UI components.
  • Responsive Design: Content adapts seamlessly to various screen sizes and zoom levels, aided by CSS container queries for component-level adaptability.

Engage with Us

We are constantly looking to improve our platform. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for new stories, please reach out to us at hello@storycast.example.com.
